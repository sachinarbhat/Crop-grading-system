Follow the steps to Recognize your crop and keep the background white

There are two methods to recognize your crop using this application

Click image

Here you have to click an image of a crop from application.

  1. First you have to take a crop which you want to recognize.
  2. Adjust the image of crop into the fixed frame which is shown.
  3. Take a picture keeping the background white.
  4. Click on the button Start Recognition which is shown below.
  5. You will get the grade of your crop below

Choose image

Here you have to Choose particular image path of the crop.

  1. First you have to select images which you want to recognise.
  2. Copy the selected image path.
  3. Paste the path into the text box.
  4. Click Recognise button shown beside it.
  5. You will get the grade of your crop below

Note: For better detection use both methods for the same crop
